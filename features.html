Features

A sharper workspace for serious studying.

Study Planner combines document analysis, study-time planning, reminders, and grounded PDF chat in one clean dashboard.

Structured PDF Summary

Summaries prioritize useful academic sections like abstract, objectives, procedure, results, and conclusion.

Document Chat

Ask about objectives, materials, results, procedures, definitions, or any topic inside your uploaded file.

Suggested Questions

The chat reads the selected PDF and creates one-click prompts for overview, objectives, reasoning, quiz practice, and revision order.

Study Time Logic

Difficulty and time estimates use document length, sentence density, topic complexity, and review time.

Reminder Console

Save a daily study time, session length, focus goal, and browser notification preference from the dashboard.
